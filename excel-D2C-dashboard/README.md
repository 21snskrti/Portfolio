##Business Performance Dashboard

## Overview
Interactive Excel dashboard analysing 5 months of sales data 
for Elara, a D2C handcrafted jewellery brand founded in April 2024.
Built to answer key business questions around revenue trends, 
city-wise performance, and product profitability.

## Business Problem
As founder, I had no structured visibility into which city, 
product, or month was driving the most revenue or margin.

## Tools Used
Microsoft Excel 2019 — INDEX-MATCH, Pivot Tables, 
Dynamic Slicers, Conditional Formatting, KPI Design

## Skills Demonstrated
- Relational data modelling across 3 linked sheets
- Cross-sheet INDEX-MATCH lookups
- Pivot Table analysis across 4 dimensions
- Dynamic slicer connected to 4 Pivot Tables simultaneously
- KPI framework: Revenue, AOV, Gross Margin %, Unique Customers
- Conditional formatting for margin pattern visualisation

## Key Findings
- August 2024 was peak revenue month
- Earrings category had the strongest gross margin profile
- AOV remained stable at approximately ₹380 across 5 months
- 6 out of 10 customers placed repeat orders within 5 months
